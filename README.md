

 📊 Business Sales Performance Analysis and Dashboard

📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to generate meaningful insights using **Power BI**.
The dashboard helps stakeholders understand sales performance, profitability, and customer trends to make data-driven decisions.

---

 🎯 Objectives

* Identify **top-performing categories and regions**
* Analyze **sales trends over time**
* Evaluate **shipping performance**
* Understand **profit and discount impact**
* Provide actionable business insights

---

 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** (Data Visualization)
* **Excel / CSV Dataset**
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**

---

📊 Dashboard Features

🔹 KPI Cards

* 💰 Total Profit: **$14.64K**
* 💵 Total Revenue: **$774.67K**
* 📦 Total Orders: **790**
* 🏷️ Average Discount: **15.68%**

---

### 📈 Sales Trend Analysis

* Year-wise sales trend visualization
* Identifies growth and decline patterns

---

📦 Category-wise Sales

* Technology generates highest revenue
* Followed by Furniture and Office Supplies

---

🚚 Ship Mode Analysis

* Standard Class has the highest sales
* Same Day shipping has the lowest usage

---

 🌍 Region-wise Sales

* Sales distributed across East, West, Central, and South
* East region contributes the highest share

---
🧠 Key Insights

* 📌 **Technology category** is the top revenue generator
* 📌 **Standard shipping** dominates sales
* 📌 Sales dipped in 2016 but increased significantly in 2017
* 📌 Discount levels may be impacting profitability

---

💡 Recommendations

* Focus on **high-performing categories (Technology)**
* Improve **Same Day shipping strategy**
* Optimize **discount policies** to increase profit
* Expand operations in **high-performing regions**
